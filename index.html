Who is Metaphor

Metaphor is a Call of Duty Warzone streamer who previously held the world record in solo kills. His playstyle can be described as aggressive and reckless, and he does not shy away from a fight.

While this playstyle often leads him to lose games, it makes for entertaining gameplay and often high kill games. As a streamer he prioritizes his chat and does everything he can to interract with his viewers at an impressive rate.
